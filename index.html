Hello from AMP!

Today we are participating in a mapping exercise about how we experience our neighborhood.

This project, organized by UW-Tacoma, will inform professors and researchers on how we use certain spaces and avoid others. This information will then be turned into a report, so that our voices are heard, understood, and shared with organizations like the Parks department and city government.

The survey consists of 8 map-based questions and some questions about you. Please follow along using the buttons on the left side of the screen. Double tap to zoom in.
